Our Mission

Turning hard days into hopeful ones.

Adams Infinite Legacy is a California nonprofit public benefit corporation dedicated to supporting individuals and families navigating serious and chronic illness. We provide financial assistance, wellness resources, and moments of community that remind people they are not alone.

The Get Well Soon Wellness Festival is our signature annual event — a free, family-friendly gathering of health screenings, healing arts, movement, music, food, and local resources. Your sponsorship makes the day possible and directly funds our year-round assistance programs.

# VIDEO 1 — "What is Quorum?" (Marketing, ~90 seconds)

**Purpose:** make a nonprofit founder feel understood, show the product, send them to quorumsuite.com.
**Audience:** someone who just decided to start a nonprofit (or runs a small volunteer board) and is dreading the paperwork.
**Tone:** warm, competent, founder-to-founder. Never corporate, never legalistic, never hypey.
**Voice rules:** words to use — *guide, plain English, in order, on the record, one place*. Words to avoid — *disrupt, revolutionary, guaranteed, legal advice*.
**Brand:** terracotta/cream warmth; serif-feeling calm. Music: light, optimistic, unobtrusive.
**Hard rules:** Quorum organizes and guides — it is NOT a law firm and never files anything for the user. Say "27 steps across 5 stages" (never 38/7). Don't quote exact prices; say "founding-member pricing, locked for life."

## Script (narration + visuals)

**[0:00–0:12] THE PAIN — dark/cream title cards, no product yet**
> Starting a nonprofit sounds beautiful — until the paperwork shows up. State filings. IRS forms. Bylaws. Board votes. Seven signatures on everything. And nobody hands you a checklist.

*Visual: text cards of the words "Articles. EIN. Bylaws. 1023. Signatures." piling up.*

**[0:12–0:24] THE TURN — first product reveal**
> Quorum is the private portal that takes your foundation from "we have an idea" to a fully compliant 501(c)(3) — without the chaos.

*Visual: `scene-1-dashboard.png` — slow push-in on the progress ring, then the KPI row.*

**[0:24–0:40] THE ROADMAP**
> It starts with the roadmap the government never sends you: twenty-seven steps across five stages, in the order the law actually works — each one explained in plain English. Where to go, what it costs, what deadline you can't miss.

*Visual: `scene-2-checklist.png` — pan down the steps; zoom on one step's gray help text so it's readable.*

**[0:40–0:55] PAPERWORK & SIGNATURES**
> Every governance document lives in one library — explained in plain language, and routed to your whole board for legal e-signature. You always know who's signed and who's holding things up.

*Visual: `scene-3-documents.png` — zoom on a status badge ("Awaiting signatures"), then an "Open in DocuSeal" button.*

**[0:55–1:10] DECISIONS ON THE RECORD**
> When the board needs to decide, you put it to a vote. Every director votes For, Against, or Abstain — from their phone, from their inbox — and the result is on the record. A passed motion can even draft its own formal resolution for your attorney to review.

*Visual: `scene-4-votes.png` — zoom on the tally bar, then `scene-5-email.png` — the email a director receives, zoom on the "Review & vote in the portal" button.*

**[1:10–1:20] MEETINGS**
> Board meetings and filing deadlines share one calendar — one click and you're in the Zoom room.

*Visual: `scene-6-calendar.png` — brief; zoom on a "Join Zoom" button.*

**[1:20–1:30] PROOF + CTA**
> Quorum wasn't invented in a boardroom. It was built launching a real California nonprofit — and now it's yours. Start free at quorumsuite dot com. Founding members lock their pricing for life.

*Visual: back to `scene-1-dashboard.png`, settle; end card: "Quorum — The Nonprofit Board OS · quorumsuite.com · Start free".*

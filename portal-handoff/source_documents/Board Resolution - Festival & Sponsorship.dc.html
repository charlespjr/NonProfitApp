Resolution & Written Consent of the Board of Directors

Approval of the Get Well Soon Wellness Festival and Sponsorship Program

The undersigned, constituting the Board of Directors of Adams Infinite Legacy (the “Corporation”), acting at a duly called meeting at which a quorum was present, or by written consent in lieu of a meeting, hereby adopt the following resolutions:

WHEREAS, the Corporation is organized to support individuals and families facing serious and chronic illness through financial assistance, wellness resources, and community programs; and

WHEREAS, the Board wishes to establish the Get Well Soon Wellness Festival as an official annual program of the Corporation in furtherance of its charitable mission; and

WHEREAS, the Board wishes to fund the Festival and the Corporation's programs in part through a tiered corporate and community sponsorship program; and

WHEREAS, the Board has reviewed the proposed sponsorship levels, benefits, and recognition set forth in the Sponsorship Prospectus presented to the Board;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board hereby approves and establishes the Get Well Soon Wellness Festival as an official program of the Corporation; and

RESOLVED FURTHER, that the Board approves the sponsorship program and the following suggested sponsorship levels, subject to adjustment by the officers as needed: Presenting ($10,000), Gold ($5,000), Silver ($2,500), Community ($1,000), and In-Kind/Friend contributions; and

RESOLVED FURTHER, that the officers of the Corporation are authorized to solicit and accept sponsorships, execute sponsorship agreements, and issue written acknowledgments to sponsors consistent with the Corporation's Gift Acceptance Policy; and

RESOLVED FURTHER, that the officers are authorized to take all actions and expend funds reasonably necessary to plan and hold the Festival and carry out the intent of these resolutions.

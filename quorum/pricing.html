Billing questions

How paying works

How do I pay?

Create your organization free, then pick a plan on the Team & Access page inside the portal. You'll get a secure QuickBooks invoice that takes card or bank (ACH) payment — your portal unlocks automatically the moment payment posts.

What does the free preview include?

Signing up is free and takes about a minute. You can explore every screen — the checklist, documents, votes, calendar — before paying. Making changes and inviting your board unlocks with any paid plan.

Can I cancel? What about refunds?

Plans are month-to-month (or yearly) and you can cancel any time — cancellation stops future charges. Details, including the 7-day window on annual plans, are in our Refund Policy.

Is there nonprofit pricing?

This is nonprofit pricing — the product exists for organizations that don't have money yet. Founding members also lock today's prices for life. If cost is genuinely the blocker for your mission, email support@quorumsuite.com and talk to us.

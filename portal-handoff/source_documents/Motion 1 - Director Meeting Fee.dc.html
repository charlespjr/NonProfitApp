Amendment to Director Compensation Policy — $200 Meeting Fee

The undersigned, constituting the Board of Directors of Adams Infinite Legacy (the “Corporation”), acting at a duly called meeting at which a quorum was present, or by written consent in lieu of a meeting, adopt the following:

WHEREAS, at formation the Board resolved that directors would serve without compensation; and

WHEREAS, the Corporation now has sufficient revenue and the Board wishes to provide a modest, reasonable per-meeting fee recognizing directors' time and service;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board amends its prior no-compensation resolution and authorizes a fee of $200 per director for each duly held board meeting attended, effective ____________; and

RESOLVED FURTHER, that the Board finds this amount just and reasonable and not excessive for a nonprofit of this size; that directors otherwise continue to serve as volunteers reimbursed only for actual expenses; and that this fee will be reviewed annually and paid only as the budget allows.

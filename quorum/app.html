Quorum on your phone

Get the app — no app store required

Quorum installs straight from your browser onto any iPhone, iPad, or Android phone. Same portal, same login, full screen, its own icon — and it opens even when your connection is spotty.

Install in about 15 seconds

Open app.quorumsuite.com on your phone, then follow the two steps for your device below. Board members can do the same — voting and signing from the home screen.

iOS iPhone & iPad
  1. 1
    Open app.quorumsuite.com in Safari (installing only works from Safari on iPhone).
  2. 2
    Tap the Share button — the square with the arrow at the bottom of the screen.
  3. 3
    Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen.”
  4. 4
    Tap Add. The Quorum icon appears on your home screen — tap it and sign in once.
Android Android phones & tablets
  1. 1
    Open app.quorumsuite.com in Chrome.
  2. 2
    Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner.
  3. 3
    Tap “Install app” (on some phones it says “Add to Home screen”).
  4. 4
    Confirm Install. Quorum shows up with your other apps — full screen, its own icon.
💻 On a computer? Chrome and Edge show a small install icon at the right end of the address bar on app.quorumsuite.com — one click puts Quorum in your dock or taskbar.

Because Quorum installs as a web app, there's nothing to download from Google Play or the App Store, updates arrive automatically, and it's always the same live portal your whole board uses.
